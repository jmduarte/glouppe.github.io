About

My name is Javier Duarte. I am an Assistant Professor in experimental high energy physics at UC San Diego and a member of the CMS collaboration at CERN. My research interests include measuring the properties and couplings of the Higgs boson and searching for beyond-the-standard-model particles in LHC data with machine learning algorithms. I am also interested in developing real-time AI for trigger systems (with applications to low-latency systems throughout science), and heterogenous computing architectures for the next generation of high energy physics experiments. For more information, visit my group's webpage: https://jduarte.physics.ucsd.edu.
