About

My name is Javier Duarte. I am a Lederman fellow in experimental high energy physics at Fermilab and a member of the CMS Collaboration at CERN. My research interests are to measure the properties and couplings of the Higgs boson and search for beyond-the-standard-model particles in LHC data. I am also interested in developing machine learning algorithms, trigger systems, and heterogenous computing architectures for the next generation of collider experiments.
