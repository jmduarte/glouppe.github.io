Call for PhD studentship in deep learning for physical sciences

The Montefiore Institute of the University of Liège (Belgium) is seeking candidates for a 4-year PhD studentship in the field of deep learning for physical sciences.

The candidate will take part in collaborative and interdisciplinary projects spanning research in deep learning, likelihood-free inference and physical sciences (such as particle physics, astronomy or geophysics), with the long-term objective of furthering scientific discovery through modern artificial intelligence methods. Research topics include deep learning for domain sciences, implicit generative models, simulator-based inference, optimal experimental design and the automation of Science at large.

Applications are invited from candidates with a master's degree (or close to completion) in Computer Science, Engineering or Data science, with a strong background in artificial intelligence and interest for physical sciences. Submit your CV, along with a brief statement of research interests and a list of references.

For applications or questions about the position, please contact me by email (g.louppe@uliege.be). The position will remain open until filled.
